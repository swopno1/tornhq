## Product: TornHQ

**Tagline:** Your Torn City Command Center — built for serious players.

**What it is:**
TornHQ is a fully custom, OLED dark-mode companion web app for Torn City (torn.com) players. It acts as the player's personal operations hub — tracking live stats, managing marketplace listings and bazaar, monitoring market prices, running faction tools, and delivering automated alerts. Built on Next.js 16, Prisma 7, Upstash Redis, and Inngest — production-grade from day one.

**Who it's for:**
Senior Torn City players who run a marketplace, bazaar, or faction and want a professional dashboard instead of juggling browser tabs and spreadsheets.

## Feature list (include all of these as bullet points or cards):

- Live dashboard: energy/nerve/happy/life bars, cooldown timers (drugs, booster, medical), hospital/jail countdowns, travel status
- Battle stats tracker: automated snapshots every 6 hours, growth chart, full history table
- Market intelligence: watchlist with price sparklines, 24-hour delta, automated alerts when items cross your price targets
- Faction command: live chain countdown with timeout warnings, sortable member activity table
- Smart alerts: background Inngest jobs poll prices every 15 min, alert drawer with unread badge
- Calculators: Torn City tools (profit margin, gym efficiency, travel cost — more added regularly)
- Security-first: Torn API keys encrypted AES-256-GCM, never exposed in logs or responses
- API proxy with Redis caching and sliding-window rate limiting (80 req/60s per user)
